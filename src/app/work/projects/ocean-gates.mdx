# Ocean Gates

A **business website** designed for **Ocean Gates**, a company specializing in **maritime logistics, shipping, and port management**.  
The site emphasizes **elegant visual design**, **clear communication of services**, and **bilingual support** (Arabic and English) to cater to a wider audience.

## Project Objective

The goal of *Ocean Gates* was to create a **professional and visually engaging online presence** that highlights the company’s services and conveys its **brand identity** clearly.  
The focus was on **bilingual usability**, **visual hierarchy**, and **subtle animations** to provide a smooth browsing experience.

## Key Features

- **Service Showcase**: Clear presentation of shipping, logistics, and port management services  
- **Bilingual Support**: Full Arabic and English interface with RTL/LTR compatibility  
- **Elegant UI**: Clean, modern design that emphasizes key messages  
- **Responsive Design**: Fully adaptive for desktop, tablet, and mobile screens  
- **Interactive Visuals**: Smooth transitions and animations to enhance engagement  

## Design Approach

The design focused on a **minimalist and visually refined style**, ensuring users can quickly understand the services offered.  
Visual elements and animations were carefully applied to **guide attention** while maintaining a clean and professional look.  
The bilingual support ensures a consistent user experience for both Arabic and English-speaking visitors, reinforcing **Ocean Gates’ brand identity**.

## Technologies Used

- **Frontend**: Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS  
- **Deployment**: Vercel  
- **Visual Content**: Optimized images, subtle animations, and bilingual typography  

## Project Highlights

*Ocean Gates* demonstrates expertise in designing **professional B2B websites** with **enhanced visual experiences** and **bilingual support**, delivering a polished online presence for maritime logistics services.
