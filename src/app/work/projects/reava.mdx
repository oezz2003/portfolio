# Reava

A **single-product website** designed for **Reava**, a startup focused on delivering **one product at the highest quality**.  
The site emphasizes **brand storytelling**, creating a **psychological connection** between the visitor and the product through **visuals, layout, and subtle interactions**.

## Project Objective

The goal of *Reava* was to **highlight the product** while fostering **emotional engagement** with potential customers.  
The website includes a **simple cart system** and facilitates **direct communication for orders**, ensuring a streamlined experience for a small-scale startup.

## Key Features

- **Product Showcase**: Detailed presentation emphasizing quality and unique selling points  
- **Psychological Engagement**: Design elements that strengthen brand-user connection  
- **Simple Cart System**: Lightweight cart for managing interest in the product  
- **Direct Orders**: Customers place orders via direct communication with the company  
- **Responsive Design**: Fully optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile  

## Design Approach

The design followed a **minimalist and emotionally engaging approach**, focusing on **clarity, aesthetics, and storytelling**.

## Technologies Used

- **Frontend**: Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS  
- **Deployment**: Vercel  
- **Visual Content**: Optimized images, smooth transitions, and subtle animations  

## Project Highlights

*Reava* demonstrates the ability to design **single-product websites** that combine **brand storytelling, psychological engagement, and elegant UI**, creating a strong connection between users and the product.
