# Mulingo

A **business website** designed for **Mulingo**, a company specializing in **translation and transcription services**.  
The site emphasizes **elegant design**, **clear visual hierarchy**, and **precise communication** of the company’s brand messages to potential clients.

## Project Objective

The goal of *Mulingo* was to create a **clean and professional online presence** that highlights the company’s services and conveys its **brand identity** effectively.  
Focus was placed on **visual clarity**, **typography**, and **subtle animations** to draw attention to key messages without overwhelming the visitor.

## Key Features

- **Service Showcase**: Clear presentation of translation and transcription offerings  
- **Elegant UI**: Minimalist design with attention to visual hierarchy  
- **Responsive Design**: Fully adaptive for desktop, tablet, and mobile screens  
- **Brand Messaging**: Design tailored to highlight the company’s core values and services  
- **Subtle Animations**: Smooth transitions to enhance user engagement  

## Design Approach

The design followed a **minimal and precise visual style**, ensuring that visitors quickly understand the services and messaging.  
Visual elements and animations were used **strategically** to guide attention while keeping the interface clean and professional.  
The overall approach reinforced **Mulingo’s brand identity** through every section of the site.

## Technologies Used

- **Frontend**: Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS  
- **Deployment**: Vercel  
- **Visual Content**: Optimized images and subtle animations  

## Project Highlights

*Mulingo* demonstrates the ability to design **front-end websites for professional B2B services** with a focus on **brand messaging, visual elegance, and clear communication**.
